This Privacy Policy explains what information **{{ site.company_name }}** ("we", "us", "our") collects when you use the Retro Game Collection mobile app ("the App"), how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

## 1. Information we collect

We collect only the data needed to run the App. Everything below is tied to your account and stored on your behalf — we do not sell it, and we do not use it for advertising profiling.

| Data | When we collect it | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **Email address** | When you create an account | Sign-in, password reset |
| **Username** _(optional)_ | When you set or edit your profile | Display name in the App |
| **Avatar image** _(optional)_ | When you upload a photo | Profile picture |
| **User ID** | Automatically on sign-up | Linking your collection, wishlist, and subscription to your account |
| **Collection & wishlist data** | When you add games | Core App functionality (this is the data you came here to create) |
| **Purchase history** | When you subscribe to RGC Pro | Verifying your subscription status |
| **App usage events** | While you use the App | Understanding which features are used so we can improve the App. You can disable analytics in your device settings. |

We **do not** collect:

- Your real name
- Your address, phone number, or payment card details (payments are handled by Apple or Google directly)
- Your location
- Your contacts, photos (other than the avatar you choose to upload), microphone, or camera
- Any biometric data

---

## 2. How we use this information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide and maintain the App (your collection, wishlist, sign-in, Pro features)
- Process subscription purchases via Apple App Store or Google Play
- Send you transactional messages (password reset, account changes)
- Understand aggregate usage patterns to improve the App
- Comply with legal obligations

We **do not** use your data to train AI models, sell to data brokers, or share with advertisers for profiling.

| Provider | What they handle | Privacy policy |

| **Supabase** | Database (collection, wishlist, profile), authentication, avatar storage | [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy) |

## 4. Ads (free tier only)

Free users see banner ads served by **Google AdMob**. AdMob may collect device identifiers (Advertising ID, IP address) to serve and measure ads, governed by Google's privacy policy linked above. **Pro subscribers see no ads** and no data is sent to AdMob from their account.

You can limit ad tracking on iOS via *Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking* and on Android via *Settings → Google → Ads*.

---

## 5. Data retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active.

When you delete your account from within the App (Profile → Danger Zone → Delete Account), we permanently remove:

- Your profile, collection, and wishlist
- Your authentication record
- Your avatar image

Some data may persist briefly in our service providers' encrypted backups (typically up to 30 days) before being purged on their normal rotation schedule.

Analytics events recorded prior to account deletion are retained in aggregate, anonymous form for product improvement.

## 6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data (already available in-app — see *Section 5*)
- **Export** your data
- **Object** to or restrict certain processing
- **Withdraw consent** for analytics

To exercise any of these rights, email **{{ site.support_email }}**. We respond within 30 days.

EU/UK users: you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

California users (CCPA): we do not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
